==================================
= Assignment - Submit Cheatsheet =
==================================

To make sure you are keeping up with your CHEATSHEET.LNX note file, you will
submit the current version of your file. This will give us an opportunity to
give feedback before use on the midterm and final.

Submission
==========
To submit your file, simply upload CHEATSHEET.LNX to Canvas.

Rubric
======
[1 pt] Correct Submission
[1 pt] File contains something that resembles notes

REMEMBER: Assignments may have different point values in their rubrics, but are
all equally weighted for the assignment portion of your course grade.
